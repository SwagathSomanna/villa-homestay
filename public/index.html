About the Property

Tranquil Estate Stay in Coorg.

Anudina Kuteera is an estate stay set in the tranquil surroundings of Coorg, nestled among coffee and arecanut plantations with a serene lake facing view. The property features a well maintained four room villa that can be reserved as individual rooms, by floor, or as an entire private stay, making it suitable for families, couples, groups, and extended visits.

Guests can enjoy the swimming pool, open yard space, shared dining area, reliable WiFi, and dedicated bonfire and barbecue spaces. Guided fishing and off-road safari can be arranged—the safari is especially enjoyable in monsoon. As an Airbnb Superhost property, Anudina Kuteera is managed with a focus on comfort, hospitality, and relaxed estate living. Check-in is at 2 pm and check-out is at 11 am.
